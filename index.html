Zombie, Man, Gun!

A variation on the classic game Rock, Paper, Scissors. This version uses Zombie, Man, and Gun but works the same way.

Rules:
Zombie kills Man
Gun kills Zombie
Man masters Gun.
Computer will choose a random fighter.
First to win 5 battles is the victor.

Have fun!
